Exams

                    
Scheme of Work

               

     

                        

                            
Week 1: Basics of Illustration

                            
Introduction to materials and tools, Basic shapes and forms, Understanding light and shadow, Perspective drawing basics.

                        

                        

                            
Week 2: Intermediate Illustration

                            
Advanced perspective techniques, Introduction to figure drawing, Drawing from reference, Basic composition techniques.

                        

                        
                        

                        
        
                
                        

                            
Week 3: Advanced Illustration

                            
Advanced figure drawing, Dynamic composition techniques, Working with color and texture, Creating a complete illustration.

                        

                        

                            
Week 4: Digital Illustration

                            
Introduction to digital illustration tools, Digital painting techniques, Working with layers and masks, Creating digital illustrations from start to finish.

                        

                        

                            
Week 5: Project Week

                            
Students work on their final project, applying the techniques and skills learned throughout the course.

                        

                        

                            
Week 6: Presentation and Critique

                            
Students present their final projects, Peer and instructor critique, Discussion of techniques and concepts used in the projects.

                        

                        
                        

                        
                    

                

            

            






		
            
            

                

                	  	

                    
Contest

                    
Welcome to the Annual Creativity Contest!

                    
This year's contest is all about pushing the boundaries of creativity. Participants are encouraged to explore new techniques, experiment with different materials, and create something truly unique.

                    
Contest Rules:

            
                
             
   
                    
    
                        
  • Open to all students.
    • 
                        
  • Each participant can submit up to three entries.
    • 
                        
  • Entries must be original works created during the contest period.
    • 
                        
  • All entries must be submitted by the deadline.
    • 
                    

                    
Prizes:

                    
    
                        
  • 1st Prize: Sewing Machine industrial
    • 
                        
  • 2nd Prize: Sewing Machine Manual
    • 
                        
  • 3rd Prize: Sewing Supplies kits
    • 
                    

                    
Don't miss this opportunity to showcase your talent and creativity!
